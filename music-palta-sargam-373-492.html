Palta in Hindustani Classical music is a set of rhythmic notes based on a permutational pattern in ascending and descending order. Driven by the need to create Paltas for my practice and intrigued by the technical challenge it offered to my left‐brain, I wrote a comprehensive algorithm that can generate up to 4,000 Paltas with a click of a button. Released here are about 500 Paltas. Note that although all Swaras are pure and in basic Thāt Bilaval, these Paltas can be practised within any Thāt.
